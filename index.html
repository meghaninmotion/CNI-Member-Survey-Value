Key findings

Across all roles

Cross-institution collaboration and advocacy are the top two asks for both Deans (57%/53%) and Assoc Deans/Directors in digital & IT roles (57%/55%) — nearly identical priorities.

CIO outlier

CIOs prioritize advocacy at 75% — their top ask — while interest in additional workshops drops to just 13%, far below other roles. They want policy influence more than skills-building.

6–10 year members

The heaviest workshop requesters of any cohort at 58% — past orientation but still actively building skills and networks. Training also remains high at 38%.

Tenure shift

Interest in training drops sharply with tenure — from 42% among members with 0–5 years to just 16% among those with 20+ years. Meanwhile, interest in resources & research publications grows over the same range, from 31% to 36%, reflecting a gradual shift from skill-building to staying informed.
